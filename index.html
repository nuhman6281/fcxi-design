00 / VISION

What we're building

FCXI is a discovery platform for amateur football. Players build a verified profile with stats, availability, highlights and history. Clubs search, filter, evaluate and recruit — all in one app. Below is the product vision that guides every screen you're about to see.

Verified players & clubs Player stats, availability & highlights Open trials & direct messaging Location-based discovery Dual role — same account, both sides Push notifications in real time
01 / WELCOME

First launch & role selection

New users are greeted with the FCXI brand splash and are asked to choose their role — Player or Club. This single decision personalises the rest of the app, from the dashboard they see to the people they can discover.

Brand splash
The FCXI identity sets the tone — bold, energetic, football-first.
Who are you? LIGHT
A clear fork in the road — Player or Club. Each card explains the value in one line.
Player selected
Visual confirmation with the brand accent. A gentle note clarifies the choice is permanent.
Club selected
Selecting Club opens up the recruitment-side of the platform.
02 / AUTHENTICATION

Sign in & create account

Passwordless sign-in via Email or Phone OTP. New users register with a privacy agreement, returning users sign in — both paths use a six-digit one-time code with a visible ten-minute expiry. Available in light and dark modes.

Login — Email LIGHT
Returning users enter their email and get a one-time code. No passwords, no friction.
Login — Phone LIGHT
Same flow via SMS OTP for users who prefer their phone number.
Login DARK
The same flow in dark mode — the app follows the user's system preference.
Friendly error handling
Rate limits, network errors and invalid codes surface as non-blocking, plain-English toasts.
Create account
Registration requires explicit agreement to Terms of Service and Privacy Policy — tappable links open in-app.
Verify OTP LIGHT
Six-digit code entry with live resend timer and a clear expiry window.
Verify OTP DARK
Consistent verification experience across modes.
03 / PLAYER ONBOARDING

Three quick steps to a complete profile

A player's first impression matters. We break profile creation into three focused steps — personal info, football credentials, and media — with a visible progress indicator so users always know how far they are from being discoverable by clubs.

Step 1 — Basic Information
Name, date of birth, region, height and preferred foot. Only the essentials.
Step 2 — Football Details
Playing position, skill level, availability and club history — the data clubs actually search on.
Step 3 — Photos & Videos
A profile photo and a highlight reel. This is what separates a name from a memorable player.
04 / PLAYER EXPERIENCE

Home, discovery & inbound interest

The player dashboard surfaces three things at a glance: profile progress, clubs near the user, and recent activity on their profile. It answers "am I being seen?" in a single scroll.

Home LIGHT
Profile completion card, clubs near the player, and real-time activity feed.
Home DARK
Live profile views, suggested clubs and a running activity log in dark mode.
Clubs directory LIGHT
Search clubs by name or location and filter by type — Academy, Professional, Semi-Pro.
Clubs directory DARK
The same directory with dark-mode treatment and quick filter chips.
View club
Players can view a club's profile, see their stats and trophies, and tap Message Club to start a conversation.
05 / PLAYER PROFILE

Showcase, edit & media gallery

The player profile is a living CV. Photos, videos, position, club history and availability — all editable in place. The profile header shows the most important stats: photos, videos, and how many clubs have viewed them.

Profile LIGHT
A clear empty state invites new players to add media, with a direct CTA.
Profile with media DARK

Edit profile LIGHT
All fields editable inline, with validation and instant save.
Edit profile DARK
Multi-select chips for positions, availability toggles, and one-tap photo upload.
Public player view LIGHT
How a club sees a player: key stats, availability badge, and a prominent Message Player CTA.
Public player view DARK
Highlight media and club history are front and centre — ready for evaluation.
06 / CLUB SETUP

Creating a club profile

Clubs complete a short profile — name, location, contact, bio and type — so they can be discovered by players and show up in location-based search.

Club profile — empty LIGHT
A focused single-screen form with logo upload, identity fields and a club-type selector.
Club profile — filled LIGHT
Filling the form gives immediate visual feedback and an active Continue state.
Club profile DARK
The same flow in dark mode, designed for evening use.
07 / CLUB EXPERIENCE

Discovering & shortlisting players

The club-side experience is built around discovery. A search-first dashboard surfaces the newest players, one tap opens an advanced search with filters for position and availability, and each player card leads directly to a conversation.

Club home LIGHT
Key metrics at the top — Players, Chats, Saved — and a prominent Search Players action.
Club home DARK
Dark theme pushes the brand-green search button to the forefront.
Discover players LIGHT
Filter by role, availability, or free-text — results update live with player summary cards.
Discover players DARK
Sort controls and filter chips stay persistent as the club scrolls.
08 / CLUB PROFILE

Overview, members & achievements

Each club gets a full profile with three tabs: Overview (bio, contact), Members (staff and player roster — visible only to the club's own members) and Achievements (trophies, milestones, participation).

Overview LIGHT
Public club info with stats, contact and bio — the first thing a player sees.
Overview DARK
Segmented tabs let viewers move through the club's three sections smoothly.
Members DARK
Roster management — add staff, players or associates. Only visible to club members.
Achievements DARK
Trophies, milestones and competition participation — building social proof for the club.
09 / MESSAGING

Direct conversations between players & clubs

Once a club is interested, the conversation is one tap away. The inbox shows unread counts, and each chat thread delivers real-time messaging with delivery/read receipts and push notifications.

Inbox — empty LIGHT
A friendly empty state — no dead ends, always a next step.
Inbox — empty DARK
Dark-mode treatment of the empty state.
Conversation
Real-time messaging with online presence, date separators and delivery checkmarks — unread badges in brand accent keep attention where it's needed.
10 / SETTINGS & PRIVACY

Control, consent & support

A single settings screen handles profile, privacy, notifications, language, help and legal. Users decide who can see them and who can message them. Dual-role users (who are both a player and a club) can add and switch accounts here.

Settings LIGHT
Dual-role-aware — users who are both a club and a player can switch or add accounts.
Settings — Club DARK

Settings — Player DARK
From a player account, users can add a club account to expand their role.
Privacy controls LIGHT
Three simple toggles put the user in control: visibility, online status, message openness.
Privacy controls DARK
Privacy is explicit, simple and reversible.
Terms & Privacy
Full legal text, accessible in-app — the same content users agreed to at sign-up.
Help & Support
Direct support contact, common questions answered, and an always-available bug-report shortcut.
